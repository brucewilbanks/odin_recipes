Stir Fry Recipe

When the chef needs a break!

This is a very easy and quick recipe, requiring only 6 ingredients.

Stir fry is my go to dish for the family and those times where I don't wish to spend a very long time in the kitchen

Ingredients

  • (1 lb.) Stir Fry Steak
  • (1) Green Pepper
  • (1) Red Pepper
  • (1) Can Of Baby Corn
  • (1) Jar Of Stir Fry Sauce
  • (1.5 lbs) White Rice

Steps

  1. First let's drizzle oil in a wok and put that on the stove at medium heat. Add the steak into the wok.
  2. While the meat is cooking add the rice to a pot of water and cook or use a rice cooker.
  3. Slice the green and red peppers into small chunks or thin slivers, however you prefer.
  4. When the steak is mostly brown cooked add the peppers and baby corn to the wok and cook for 10 mins. Add 2 tbs of stir fry sauce while cooking every few minutes.
  5. Remove all ingredients and place in bowl to eat.
